Past Life

Past life regression is a hypnosis-based technique that claims to retrieve memories of previous lives or incarnations.

  • That Old, Familiar Feeling
  • Your Dreams Are So Real It's Like You're Actually There
  • You Got an A in History (Without Even Trying)
  • You Have a Soul Mate

Past-life memories can sometimes emerge as repeated dreams and nightmares, according to believers. Dreams about routine or everyday activities may allude to a certain location you visited in a previous life. People who show up in your dreams on a frequent basis may have had a particular relationship with you in a previous life. Similarly, nightmares might be the result of past-life tragedies that have stuck to our spirits and haunted our sleep.
