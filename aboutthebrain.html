About the Brain

You could feel you're seeing something for the first time if your initial glimpse of it, such as a view from a hillside, didn't need your whole concentration. However, even if you weren't fully conscious of what you were seeing, your brain remembers the prior perception.

  • Multitasking is impossible.
  • An adult brain weighs about 3 pounds.
  • About 75% of the brain is made up of water.
  • The human brain will triple its size the first year of life.
  • Headaches are caused by a chemical reaction.
  • The human brain contains approximately one hundred billion neurons.
